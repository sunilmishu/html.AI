ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

What is artificial intelligence?

    Artificial intelligence (AI)refers to the field of science dedicated to building computers and machines that can reason, learn, and act in ways that typically require human intelligence. These systems can perform tasks such as recognizing speech, making decisions, and identifying patterns. Essentially, AI aims to simulate human cognitive abilities using technology.
